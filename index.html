What We Do

Four pillars of environmental intelligence

From EU regulatory compliance to on-the-ground field verification — we deliver the data and analysis that nature-positive organisations need to operate with confidence.

📋
EUDR & Regulatory Compliance

We help European companies navigate the EU Deforestation Regulation, CSRD biodiversity reporting, and emerging nature-risk frameworks — backed by real field data from origin landscapes.

  • Deforestation risk assessments & supply chain traceability
  • EUDR due diligence documentation
  • CSRD / ESRS E4 biodiversity disclosures
  • EU Taxonomy alignment & TNFD / SBTN support
  • Horizon Europe & LIFE programme technical inputs
🌿
Biodiversity & Nature-Based Solutions

We design, assess, and validate biodiversity projects — from REDD+ monitoring to biodiversity credit scoping — with a scientific rigour rooted in field experience across tropical ecosystems.

  • Ecosystem assessment & biodiversity monitoring
  • Biodiversity credit scoping & verification support
  • REDD+ MRV systems & carbon project advisory
  • Assisted Natural Regeneration (ANR) planning
  • Nature-based solution feasibility studies
🤖
Environmental AI & Data Intelligence

We develop expert-level environmental datasets for frontier AI models, conduct remote sensing analysis, and deliver predictive modelling for land-use and climate risks.

  • Expert LLM training datasets for climate & ecology
  • Remote sensing analysis (Sentinel-2, Landsat, GEE)
  • Land-use change detection & predictive modelling
  • AI model evaluation for environmental reasoning
  • Data visualisation & spatial analysis reports
🛸
Field Verification & Drone Intelligence

We deploy drone-assisted surveys, GIS mapping, and ground-truth data collection across Central and West Africa — providing the field credibility that desk-based analysis cannot replicate.

  • Drone photogrammetry & aerial surveys
  • GIS mapping & spatial data collection
  • Ground-truth validation for carbon & biodiversity projects
  • Community & stakeholder engagement in field operations
  • Forest monitoring & ecosystem health assessments
