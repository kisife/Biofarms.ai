Four pillars of environmental intelligence

EUDR & Regulatory Compliance

I help European companies navigate the EU Deforestation Regulation, CSRD biodiversity reporting, and emerging nature-risk frameworks — backed by real field data from origin landscapes rather than desk estimates.

  • Deforestation risk assessments & supply chain traceability
  • EUDR due diligence documentation
  • CSRD / ESRS E4 biodiversity disclosures
  • EU Taxonomy alignment & TNFD / SBTN support
  • Horizon Europe & LIFE programme technical inputs
