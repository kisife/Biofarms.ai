The Field-to-AI Bridge

I believe organizations have a duty to embed environmental sustainability into every layer of their operations, creating value that regenerates ecosystems, reduces emissions, and supports resilient communities throughout the value chain.

The practical result: I can read a Sentinel-2 classification, walk the forest plot it represents, and identify precisely what the model got wrong. I translate that gap into verified field data usable for EUDR compliance, REDD+ MRV, carbon credit verification, and biodiversity finance reporting.

Currently, I serve as Environmental Intelligence Specialist at Cleantech Nexus (Geneva), providing field credible biodiversity and NbS intelligence for EU funded projects and emerging market due diligence across Africa and Europe. I also work as Environmental Science Expert at Alignerr and Environmental AI Data Contributor at Outlier.ai, designing expert-level evaluation datasets for frontier LLMs in climate, ecology, and forest governance.
